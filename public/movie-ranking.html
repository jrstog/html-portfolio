The Best Movies According to Jack

My top 3 movies of all-time

Jujutsu Kaisen 0

An absolutely fantastic anime movie. It has great fight scenes along with an amazing musical score that will captivate you.

The Glenn Miller Story

A really cool movie telling the story of famous trombonist and big band conductor Glenn Miller. Fantastic jazz music throughout the entire film.

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy (Extended Edition)

A masterpiece. Fantastic worldbuilding, stunning visuals, emotion provoking soundtrack. Everything you want in a movie and more.
